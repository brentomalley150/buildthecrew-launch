Step 1 · Build your crew

It starts with one person building a crew

You add your loved one and what they need, then invite the family and friends who help out. Each person says when they're free and what they're good at.

Most families have these three people — and BuildtheCrew is built for all of them:

Maya, who does most of the caring · Daniel, the sibling who lives far away · Patricia, the family friend who'd love to help.

Step 2 · The part that changes everything

Kira gives each person their own plan

Step 3 · It stays effortless

When something gets done, everyone just… knows

No one has to send the update. The loop closes by itself.
